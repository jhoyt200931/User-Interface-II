With that being the case, I turned my attention towards technology related fields and found the world of coding. It really sparked my interest and I found myself wanting to learn all about this world. I purchased a "Coding for Dummies" book and started teaching myself how to code. I had learned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript by the time I felt like I was getting out of my depth with self education. I them decided to turn towards professional sources, thus began my coding journey at Lambda School!
